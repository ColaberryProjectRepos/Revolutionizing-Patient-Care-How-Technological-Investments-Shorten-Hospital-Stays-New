Background 1

In the rapidly evolving healthcare sector, the advent of sophisticated technologies such as MRI Units, CT Scanners, and advanced hospital beds has opened new frontiers in patient care. These investments are not just enhancements to the medical infrastructure but pivotal elements in transforming patient outcomes and operational efficiencies.

Problem Statement

At the heart of healthcare delivery challenges lies the critical issue of prolonged hospital stays, which not only escalates healthcare costs but also limits the availability of essential medical resources. A notable 30% of hospitals grapple with optimizing these technological investments, directly impacting patient throughput and satisfaction.

Objectives
